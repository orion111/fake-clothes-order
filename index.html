Privacy Policy — Dripcart

Last updated: 4 July 2026

Dripcart ("the app") is a shopping simulator — a parody/entertainment app. It involves no real products, no real money, no real payments, and no real shipping. This policy explains the limited data the app handles.

What the app collects

You provide (optional):

  • Display name — a nickname you type during onboarding.
  • City — a text label you type during onboarding (free text you type; the app does not read your device's GPS or location).
  • Avatar — an emoji you pick.

Generated automatically:

  • Anonymous account ID — when you open the leaderboard, the app creates an anonymous account (via Supabase Anonymous Sign-In) identified by a random ID. It is not linked to your email, phone number, Google account, or real identity.

The app does not collect: real names, email, phone, contacts, precise/coarse device location, or payment/financial information. (An advertising ID is used by our ads provider — see Advertising below.)

How it's used and shared

  • Your display name, city, avatar, and Drip Score are uploaded to our backend (Supabase) to power the in-app public leaderboard. These are visible to other users of the app. Please don't enter anything private as your display name or city.
  • Everything else (catalog browsing, bag, orders, closet) stays on your device.
  • Data sent to the backend is transmitted over HTTPS (encrypted in transit).
  • We do not sell your data. Aside from the advertising ID used to serve ads (see Advertising), we do not share your data with advertisers.

Advertising

The app shows an optional rewarded video ad (you choose to watch it for a bonus in-app spin) served by Google AdMob. To serve and measure ads, Google and its partners may collect and use your device's advertising ID and related information. This ID is a resettable identifier you control in your device settings (Settings → Google → Ads), where you can reset it or opt out of ad personalization. See Google's Privacy & Terms for how Google uses this data. No ad is shown unless you tap to watch one.

Photos & fictional content

All product photos are stock imagery provided via Pexels (used under the Pexels License) and are shown for illustration only. Every product, brand name, price, review, rating and "haul" in the app is fictional and procedurally generated — nothing depicted is a real item for sale. People appearing in stock photos are models and do not endorse, sponsor, or have any connection to the app or its fictional products. Any resemblance to a real brand, product, or person is incidental. If you believe a specific photo raises an intellectual-property or likeness concern, contact us (below) and we will remove it.

Third-party services

  • Supabase (backend/database for the leaderboard) — see supabase.com/privacy.
  • Pexels (product photos load from the Pexels image CDN) — see pexels.com/privacy.
  • Google AdMob (optional rewarded ads) — see policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

Notifications

The app may send a local notification ("your haul arrived") for the in-app delivery simulation. You can deny or revoke the notification permission at any time in system settings.

Data retention & deletion

Your leaderboard row persists until deleted. To delete your data, email the address below with your display name, or clear the app's data / uninstall the app to remove on-device data.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13.
